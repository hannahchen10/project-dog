About us.

We are a non-profit organisation who aims to care for as many dogs as possible.

Our Mission
Project Dog is an organisation that works with invididuals and kennels to help ensure the safety and wellbeing of local dogs. We will never reject a healthy dog and we aim to permanently rehome dogs but work closely with our volunteers who provide vital support on a day to day basis.
